About

Dot Dash

A fun and interactive way to learn Morse Code

Dot Dash makes learning Morse Code simple, fun, and interactive. A reimagined edition of MorseCode, the Swift Student Challenge 2025 winning app, Dot Dash transforms Morse learning into a modern, engaging experience.

The app is built around three core modes: Learn, Practice, and Play. Explore Morse patterns and understand how dots and dashes form letters in Learn. Improve speed and recognition through guided exercises in Practice. Then put your skills to the test in Play, where challenges like Secret Agent turn Morse decoding into an exciting mission.

With a clean design and intuitive interactions, Dot Dash helps anyone discover the language of dots and dashes — whether you're a beginner or simply curious about Morse Code.

Learn to read the rhythm of dots and dashes.
